# Step 04: Research Patterns and Choose Architecture Strategy

## Purpose

Use relevant patterns and constraints to choose the architecture strategy.

## Entry Criteria

- Step 03 exit criteria are met
- the user has confirmed or refined the current-system context

## Actions

1. Identify internal and external patterns relevant to the HLD.
2. For SketchUp-specific architecture, prefer official SketchUp guidance and established extension patterns.
3. Compare plausible architecture strategies at a high level.
4. Choose the strategy that best satisfies the source drivers and constraints.
5. Capture the rationale for the chosen strategy and any rejected alternatives worth noting.

## Expected Outputs

- relevant pattern summary
- chosen architecture strategy
- rationale for the strategy
- notable rejected alternatives

## Exit Criteria

- the HLD has a clear architecture strategy
- the strategy is grounded in real patterns and constraints
- the rationale is strong enough for the user to confirm or refine

## Confirmation Gate

Present the architecture strategy and rationale, then ask the user for confirmation before proceeding to Step 05.
