# Step 08: Validate Architecture Posture and Open Questions

## Purpose

Validate that the document is a real HLD rather than a PRD restatement or a task plan.

## Entry Criteria

- Step 07 exit criteria are met

## Actions

1. Validate the HLD against the source PRD, domain analysis, and adjacent HLDs.
2. Check for mixed scope between platform and capability concerns.
3. Confirm that the document is architectural rather than product-spec or task-plan content.
4. Capture opened questions that remain unresolved.
5. Check that links are repo-relative.

## Expected Outputs

- consistency review notes
- posture-validation notes
- opened question list
- required corrections if any

## Exit Criteria

- the HLD is internally consistent
- mixed-scope issues are resolved or made explicit
- the document reads as an HLD rather than a PRD or task plan
- opened questions are visible
- links are repo-relative

## Proceed Condition

Proceed to Step 09 once the HLD passes validation or all required corrections are applied.
