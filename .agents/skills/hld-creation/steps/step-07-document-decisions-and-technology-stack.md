# Step 07: Document Decisions and Technology Stack

## Purpose

Capture the major architectural choices and the technology decisions that support them.

## Entry Criteria

- Step 06 exit criteria are met

## Actions

1. Document the key architectural decisions.
2. For each major decision, record the reason it was chosen.
3. Define the technology stack relevant to the HLD.
4. Keep technology choices tied to architecture needs rather than listing everything in the repo.
5. Ensure the decisions are consistent with the source specs and prior steps.

## Expected Outputs

- key architectural decisions
- rationale for each decision
- technology stack summary

## Exit Criteria

- the important architecture decisions are explicit
- the technology stack is relevant and justified
- the decisions align with the source context

## Proceed Condition

Proceed to Step 08 once the HLD has its decision and stack sections materially complete.
