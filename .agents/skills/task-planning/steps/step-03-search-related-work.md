# Step 03: Search Related Work

## Purpose

Ground the plan in existing repo decisions, prior work, and relevant specifications.

## Entry Criteria

- Step 02 exit criteria are met
- the user has confirmed or refined goals and constraints

## Actions

1. Identify search terms from the task, goals, and affected components.
2. Search related tasks, HLDs, PRDs, domain-analysis content, and codebase references where relevant.
3. Read the matching documents that materially affect planning.
4. Summarize what is reusable, what constrains the task, and what gaps remain.
5. Record any decisions or assumptions that are invalidated by the research.

## Expected Outputs

- search term list
- related-work summary
- reusable decisions or patterns
- unresolved gaps after research

## Exit Criteria

- the planning effort is grounded in the most relevant existing work
- reused patterns and constraints are explicit
- unresolved gaps after research are documented

## Confirmation Gate

Present a summary of the research findings and ask the user for confirmation before proceeding to Step 04.
