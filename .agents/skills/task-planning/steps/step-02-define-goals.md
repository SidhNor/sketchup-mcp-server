# Step 02: Define Goals

## Purpose

Turn the captured problem into a clear scope boundary for planning.

## Entry Criteria

- Step 01 exit criteria are met
- the user has confirmed or refined the captured problem

## Actions

1. List measurable goals for the task.
2. Document business and technical constraints.
3. Define explicit non-goals.
4. Identify assumptions that planning currently depends on.
5. Check the goals against the task, HLD, PRD, and domain rules for consistency.

## Expected Outputs

- measurable goals
- business constraints
- technical constraints
- non-goals
- assumptions list

## Exit Criteria

- goals are measurable enough to guide implementation planning
- non-goals clearly limit task scope
- constraints are separated into business and technical concerns
- assumptions are explicit and reviewable

## Confirmation Gate

Present a concise summary and ask the user to confirm or refine the goals before proceeding to Step 03.
