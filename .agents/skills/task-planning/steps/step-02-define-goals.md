# Step 02: Define Goals

## Purpose

Turn the problem into a clear planning scope.

## Entry Criteria

- Step 01 exit criteria are met

## Actions

1. List measurable goals.
2. Document business and technical constraints.
3. Define non-goals.
4. Identify assumptions.

## Expected Outputs

- goals
- constraints
- non-goals
- assumptions

## Exit Criteria

- goals are measurable enough to guide planning
- constraints and non-goals are explicit

## Confirmation Gate

Present a summary and ask for confirmation before Step 03.
